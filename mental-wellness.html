Our Mental Wellness Services

Comprehensive support for emotional well-being and mental health

Individual Counseling

One-on-one sessions with trained counselors to address personal challenges, trauma, and emotional difficulties in a safe, confidential environment.

  • Trauma-focused therapy
  • Grief and loss counseling
  • Stress management techniques

Support Groups

Peer-led groups where community members can share experiences, find solidarity, and build resilience together.

  • Women's support circles
  • Youth resilience groups
  • Parent support networks

Creative Therapy

Art, music, and writing for emotional expression and healing

Digital Support

Anonymous online resources and virtual counseling options

Child & Family

Specialized support for children and family units
