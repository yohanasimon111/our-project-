What We Do

Transforming lives through targeted programs that address critical community needs

Hunger Help

Active

Providing access to food aid, nutrition support, and digital food request tools to fight hunger and malnutrition in Mahama Camp. Our program ensures that families have reliable access to nutritious meals through a streamlined distribution system.

500+ families supported monthly
Community gardens established
Digital food request system

Food distribution day at Mahama Camp

Youth Empowerment

Growing

Creating training programs, digital skills hubs, and job opportunities for young people to build their future. We empower youth with marketable skills and connect them with employment opportunities.

1,000+ youth trained in digital skills
Tech workshops and coding bootcamps
Job placement assistance

Youth learning digital skills at our tech hub

Mental Wellness

Support

Offering counseling, emotional support, and safe digital spaces for healing and sharing stories of resilience. We provide mental health services tailored to the unique needs of refugee communities.

300+ community members supported
