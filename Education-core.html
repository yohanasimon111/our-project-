Education in Mahama Refugee Camp

Comprehensive analysis of educational programs and vocational training opportunities

Overview

Mahama Refugee Camp in Rwanda hosts approximately 60,000 refugees, with over 60% being children under 18. Education is a fundamental right and a critical component of refugee protection and empowerment. Multiple organizations collaborate to provide comprehensive educational services from early childhood through vocational training, following both Rwandan and Burundian curricula to accommodate the diverse refugee population.
