Donation FAQ

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, HopeLink Mahama is a registered non-profit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a receipt for your records.

How much of my donation goes to programs?

98% of every donation goes directly to program services. Only 2% is used for administrative costs and payment processing fees.

Can I cancel my monthly donation?

Yes, you can cancel your monthly donation at any time by contacting us at donations@hopelink.mahama.org or through your donor portal.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, bank transfers, mobile money (M-Pesa, Airtel Money), and cryptocurrency.

Will I receive updates about my donation's impact?

Yes, all donors receive quarterly impact reports showing how their contributions are making a difference. Monthly donors receive additional updates.

How can my company get involved?

We offer corporate partnerships, employee matching programs, sponsorships, and in-kind donation opportunities. Contact partnerships@hopelink.mahama.org for more information.
