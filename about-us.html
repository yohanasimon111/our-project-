Our Story

HopeLink Mahama was founded in 2025 by a group of passionate young developers from Mahama Refugee Camp who witnessed firsthand the struggles of their community. We saw children without access to quality education, women facing barriers to opportunity, and youth without digital skills to build their futures.

Rather than accepting these challenges, we decided to turn technology into a tool for change. What started as a small coding initiative has grown into a comprehensive digital platform connecting refugees and host communities to essential services.

Our journey began with just five laptops and a shared vision. Today, we've trained over 1,000 youth in digital skills, provided food assistance to hundreds of families, and created safe spaces for mental wellness support.
